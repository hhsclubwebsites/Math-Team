Wisconsin Math League (WML) Contests

We take the WML tests the second Tuesday of every month. The tests each consist of 6 questions each worth 1 point. Top scorers are determined by cumulative score at the end of the season.

American Mathematics Competitions (AMC)

The AMC 10 and 12 are open to 9-10th and 11-12th graders respectively. They're 75-minute, 25-question tests that can qualify you for more advanced competitions, such as AIME and USAMO.
